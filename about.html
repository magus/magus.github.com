Noah Jorgenson

You can find Mr. Jorgenson on Twitter or send him an email.

Noah works at The University of Tulsa. When he's not doing that, he's blogging, hacking on code, and filming videos of 3D CGI he build with his bare mouse and keyboard. He would cease to function correctly without daily doses of anime and music.

Noah has also been working on SEREBRO since 2007. It's amassed multiple publications over the past few years. SEREBRO has been featured at conferences such as the Internation Conference on Software Engineering (ICSE) and was funded by the National Science Foundation.

If you have any questions or feedback, check out the issues on on GitHub. It's sort of like an "Ask Me Anything" setup, except it uses a nerdy social network and crams it into a bug tracker.
